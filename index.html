Biography / 简介

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Anhui University. I conduct research within Professor Jin Tang’s team, under the supervision of Associate Professor Xiao Wang and Professor Bo Jiang. My research interests include event-based visual perception, multimodal learning, computer vision, and artificial intelligence.

Specifically, my research topics include event camera-based and multimodal visual object tracking, human action recognition, sign language translation, and pedestrian attribute recognition. I am particularly interested in leveraging the advantages of event cameras, such as high temporal resolution, high dynamic range, and low latency, to address the limitations of conventional RGB-based vision methods under challenging conditions, including low illumination, motion blur, and complex backgrounds. My work further aims to promote the application of event-based vision in human-centric perception, intelligent surveillance, and multimodal understanding.

我目前在安徽大学计算机科学与技术学院攻读博士学位，依托汤进教授团队开展相关研究，师从王逍副教授和江波教授。 主要研究方向包括人工智能、计算机视觉、多模态学习和事件视觉感知等。

具体研究课题包括基于事件相机和多模态信息融合的视觉目标跟踪、人类动作识别、手语翻译、行人属性识别等。 主要关注如何利用事件相机高时间分辨率、高动态范围和低延迟等特性，解决传统 RGB 视觉方法在低光照、运动模糊、 复杂背景等场景下面临的挑战，并进一步推动事件相机在人中心感知和多模态理解等方向中的应用。

Research Interests / 研究方向

事件视觉感知

研究事件相机在低光照、快速运动和高动态范围场景下的感知优势与建模方法。

事件相机与 RGB 融合

探索事件流与 RGB 图像、视频及其他模态信息的协同建模与应用落地。

计算机视觉

聚焦视觉目标跟踪、动作识别、行人属性识别和视觉表示学习等任务。

人工智能与多模态学习

关注多模态信息融合、跨模态互补建模以及复杂场景下的智能感知方法。
