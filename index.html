Biography / 简介

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Anhui University. My research interests include artificial intelligence, computer vision, multimodal learning, and event-based visual perception. My research mainly focuses on event cameras and multimodal visual intelligence, aiming to explore the complementary information among event streams, RGB images/videos, and other modalities, thereby improving the perception capability and robustness of visual systems in complex real-world scenarios.

Specifically, my research topics include event camera-based and multimodal visual object tracking, human action recognition, sign language translation, and pedestrian attribute recognition. I am particularly interested in leveraging the advantages of event cameras, such as high temporal resolution, high dynamic range, and low latency, to address the limitations of conventional RGB-based vision methods under challenging conditions, including low illumination, motion blur, fast motion, and complex backgrounds. My work further aims to promote the application of event-based vision in human-centric perception, intelligent surveillance, and multimodal understanding.

我目前在安徽大学计算机科学与技术学院攻读博士学位，主要研究方向包括人工智能、计算机视觉、多模态学习和事件视觉感知等。 研究重点围绕事件相机与多模态视觉智能展开，致力于探索事件流、RGB 图像与视频以及其他模态信息之间的互补关系， 以提升视觉系统在复杂真实场景下的感知能力与鲁棒性。

具体研究课题包括基于事件相机和多模态信息融合的视觉目标跟踪、人类动作识别、手语翻译、行人属性识别等。 我关注如何利用事件相机高时间分辨率、高动态范围和低延迟等特性，解决传统 RGB 视觉方法在低光照、运动模糊、 快速运动和复杂背景等场景下面临的挑战，并进一步推动事件视觉在人中心感知、智能监控和多模态理解等方向中的应用。
